Encode Decode

Usage:

This web app embeds your message into a PNG image, making your message hidden to the normal eye!

Encode:

  1. Go to encode, upload your image (the image in which you want to hide your message), enter your message, and submit.
  2. Download the encoded PNG image.
  3. Use this image as a secret message.

Decode:

  1. Go to decode and upload your encoded image.
  2. Submit and you will get your hidden message!
